Organizational notes should be consistent and unique from other notes. Some methods could be a unique symbol, a unique type style, or a builtin method like tags. 
Examples
- [[INDEX]]
	- `#TO/DO`
		- `#TO/DO/CONCEPTUALIZE `
		- `#TO/DO/WRITE`
		- `#TO/DO/PROGRAM`
	- `#TO/EXPLORE/READ`
	- `#TO/EXPLORE/WATCH`
	- `#TO/EXPLORE/LISTEN `
